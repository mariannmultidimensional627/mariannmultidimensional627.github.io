## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

Users sometimes encounter connection errors. Check these points if you have trouble:

- Verify that both computers have an active network connection.
- Check if your firewall blocks the application. You must allow traffic on the port you selected.
- Ensure the TightVNC server runs on the host computer.
- Restart the TightVNC service if the connection drops. You can do this through the "Services" panel in Windows. 
- Type "services.msc" in the Windows search bar to open the list of services. Find TightVNC in the list and click "Restart".

## 🔒 Security tips

Remote access tools grant control over your system. Follow these practices to keep your machine safe:

- Close the viewer software as soon as you finish your work.
- Use a VPN if you must connect to your computer over the open internet. A VPN creates a secure tunnel for your data.
- Keep your Windows installation updated to protect against vulnerabilities.
